ABOUT US

The MAY Shoes was founded in May 2021 by three peer partners, Mohamad Akmalrizan Yusri bin Mahazan, Nur Anati Hamizah binti Ramdan and Nur Zulaikha binti Zul Azmi who are also university students and starting to get used to business of custom painted shoes. We provide shoe designing services as per customer's request. Each pair of shoes is custom hand-painted by us. We are open to any and all design ideas you may have! Our shoes are only available for purchase online, and they are shipped from our Shoefinity Studio in Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur.
